## What is Galaxy Brain?

Galaxy Brain is the knowledge layer of the Logos Liber ecosystem. While other systems focus on rendering, agents, or interfaces, Galaxy Brain handles the fundamental challenge: structuring the world's information into something machines can understand and humans can use.

Think of it as a universal mind — a system that can ingest any data source, parse its structure, build knowledge graphs, and serve that knowledge to any application that needs it.

The name comes from internet culture: "galaxy brain" thinking that expands beyond conventional boundaries. The project matches the ambition.

---

## What Galaxy Brain Does

**Universal Data Ingestion**
Consume data from any source: documents, databases, APIs, scraped content, structured or unstructured. Galaxy Brain doesn't care about format — it cares about meaning.

**Intelligent Parsing**
Automatic structure detection and extraction. From raw text to rich metadata, Galaxy Brain identifies entities, relationships, and patterns without manual schema definition.

**Knowledge Graph Construction**
Transform isolated data into connected knowledge. Build semantic relationships, traverse concept networks, and surface insights that emerge from connections.

**Structured Retrieval**
Query knowledge semantically, not just syntactically. Find what you mean, not just what you say. Serve structured data to any application that needs context.

**Ecosystem Integration**
Feed Generous for visualization. Power Agents of Empire with contextual knowledge. Provide data pipelines for any AI application that needs structured information.
